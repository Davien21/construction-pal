Core Features

Creative team work

Create a project team for each project, add team members and client from your phone contacts and email, assign roles and set privileges for each role, create a program of work and allow team members to contribute every step of the project. Software you didn’t realize you needed until we built it.

Track Site Inventory

Keep track of your site on the go, monitor material usage and be notified when supplies are low. Add or remove new equipments or materials categories and choose whether or not your client should have access to inventory records.

Progress Reporting

Upload and share progress photos, videos and reports with team members across all devices in real time. Of course this is possible with any other social media app but constructionpal ensures that all shared resources are stored and organized by project. This means that you do not have to worry about resources shared through different social platforms. A trial will convince you.

Share files With Team Members & client

Project leads and team members can upload and edit drawings and other resources for easy Access from mobile devices anywhere, any time; Keep every team member in the loop when drawings are revised, team members can comment and discuss drawings in an interactive thread-like manner on the go. Choose whether to allow your client access to the drawings and comments or not.

Dynamic P.O.W Creator

Our interactive app allows clients and project leads to set work targets by sliding time bars to fixate task commencement and expected completion dates, creating a simplified version of the program of work for each member to follow. Set notifications to help you remember these dates and allow team members to comment on P.O.W. A project calendar is always displayed on every team members’ dashboard on successful login to make sure no one ever misses a work date.

Payments And Accounting

Fund your construction-pal wallet from any bank to easily make work related payments that are recorded on per project basis. Team members and vendors can generate payment invoices or purchase-orders and share to each other for internal work payments. Project creator / project lead can generate financial reports periodically and share same with clients within the same platform. All transactions are logged according to purpose, beneficiary, time, date, amount and receipts are copied to both parties.

Bill Of Quantities

Project leads, approved team members and clients can view and discuss B.O.Q from anywhere on the move. How often have you needed to refer to a project bill and couldn’t immediately find it in your mail? If only you had constructionpal. ..have your project documents including bills at the tip of your fingers even from your bathroom.

G.P.S Enabled Market Place

Our ground-breaker feature, marketplace inside constructionpal helps you window-shop for construction related vendors and products from the comfort of your palm. Turn on your mobile phone G.P.S and get connected with vendors close to you, anywhere in the world. Architects can order specialized services such as 3D and 4D visualization from our innovative just3dit platform. Connect to over 1,000 ready visualization experts to get your 2D transformed to 3D in less than 24 hours.

In-Messaging

Discuss project matters in the right forum. Rest assured that chat histories related to your projects are safe and secure in one place for future references. Enjoy all the features of your favorite messaging apps plus the convenience of organized sorting.

Comprehensive resource storage

Store drawings, notes, receipts or other files for one or many projects safely and all-inone-place for easy retrieval, sharing or printing. No more scattered receipts and payment history.

Access on-the-palm

Take your projects with you everywhere you go, never miss an appointment or notification on all projects. Experience the power of global team connectivity from the palm of your hands.

Client listen-in

Another groundbreaking innovative feature, client listen-in, allows project leads to add the client as part of the team and choose whether to allow him access progress reports or not. Clients may also double as project creator(leader).
